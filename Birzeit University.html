Brief about Birzeit University

Birzeit University is a Palestinian university located in the town of Birzeit. She played a pioneering role in political events in Palestine and Birzeit University dates back to 1924 when it was founded as a primary school by Nabiha Nasser (1891-1951) in the town of Birzeit. The university started as a school and the main goal of the school was to provide primary education opportunities for girls from Birzeit and the neighboring villages at a time when schools were virtually absent in that region. Ratiba Shukair (1881-1957) was the first principal of the school and worked there until 1932. It has expanded and continues to this day to include many different specializations in different colleges.
